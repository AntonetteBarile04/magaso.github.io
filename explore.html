SWIMMING POOL

    -Mag-aso Falls Resort has a refreshing swimming pool that is suited for anyone.

    The pool water is naturally from a running water from the falls filling the infinity pool.

NIGHT COTTAGES

    -If you wanted to enjoy the night life at the resort, you can stay at

    the night cottage for P1000 good for 4 persons.Maximum of 10 persons can fit in the room.

    The room has only two beds.If want extra foam they charge P200 in case you need more.

BRIDGES AND GARDEN

    -If you need to relax, Mag-aso falls has a eye-soothing garden with

    gold fishes and lotus flowers.Also concrete bridges good for photo shoots.

FALLS

    -A 75-foot waterfall,release smoke like shower of mist as

    it cascade down the natural pool with its turquoise green water.
